Company Profile

KIKLOP Design&Engineering is a Turkey(Istanbul) based corporation owned and operated by Partners Meltem OZ, AIA , Talat EMEL , PE and Omer EMEL. Kiklop employs a staff of licensed professional archi- tects, engineers, interior designers, technical support and administrative personnel.
